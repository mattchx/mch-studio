# Script: Marketing Production Rhythm

## Short Video

### Beat 1

Voiceover: Most small businesses do not need more marketing ideas.

On-screen text: The ideas are already there.

Visual: Scattered notes showing customer questions, service ideas, seasonal reminders, and half-written drafts.

### Beat 2

Voiceover: The problem is that useful ideas rarely make it all the way to published.

On-screen text: The missing piece is rhythm.

Visual: The scattered notes organize into a simple sequence: capture, decide, draft, clean up, publish.

### Beat 3

Voiceover: One useful idea can become one post, one short script, or one clear offer every week.

On-screen text: One idea. One finished asset.

Visual: A single idea card turns into a polished post card and a small checklist with completed steps.

### Beat 4

Voiceover: That is where marketing momentum starts: practical execution, repeated consistently.

On-screen text: Publish every week.

Visual: The final asset appears in a clean social feed mockup with MCH Projects branding.
