# Brief: Marketing Production Rhythm

## Status

Status: active

## Raw Idea

Most small businesses do not have a marketing problem because they lack ideas. They have a marketing problem because useful ideas never become finished, published assets. The message should reposition MCH Projects around execution rhythm: turning raw ideas into clear posts, scripts, offers, and publishing handoffs.

## Goal

Publish a client-facing MCH Projects position piece explaining that marketing momentum comes from a repeatable production rhythm, not from waiting for the perfect idea or perfect campaign.

## Audience

Local service businesses, founders, and operators who know they should be posting and promoting more consistently, but struggle to turn ideas into finished marketing.

## CTA

Start by building a simple rhythm that turns one useful idea into one published asset every week.

## Constraints

- Keep the language plain and execution-focused.
- Avoid internal MCH Studio/app/Supabase details in the public post.
- Make it appealing to prospective MCH Projects clients.
- The first success metric is a published asset, not a saved draft.

## References

- Conversation decision: "we just need results and a way to organize the workflow."
- Reframe decision: make this client-facing, not builder-meta.
